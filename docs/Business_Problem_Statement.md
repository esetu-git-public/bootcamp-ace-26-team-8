# Business Problem Statement

Financial institutions face significant challenges when approving loan applications because there is always a risk that some borrowers may fail to repay their loans. Traditional manual assessment methods are often time-consuming, inconsistent, and may not accurately identify high-risk applicants. As a result, banks can experience financial losses due to loan defaults while also delaying the loan approval process for genuine customers.

The objective of this project is to develop a machine learning-based Loan Default Prediction system that analyzes historical borrower data and predicts whether a loan applicant is likely to default. By identifying high-risk borrowers before loan approval, the system can assist financial institutions in making informed lending decisions, reducing financial risk, improving operational efficiency, and supporting responsible lending practices.
